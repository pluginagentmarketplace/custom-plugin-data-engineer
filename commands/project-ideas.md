## Skill Levels

**Beginner** (Months 1-3)
- Single technology focus
- 1-2 week projects
- Clear requirements

**Intermediate** (Months 3-9)
- 2-3 technologies
- 2-4 week projects
- Some ambiguity

**Advanced** (Months 9+)
- Full-stack projects
- 4-8 week projects
- Real-world complexity

## Intermediate Projects

1. **End-to-End ETL Pipeline** (3 weeks)

   - Data quality checks
   - **Skills:** Airflow, Spark, testing
